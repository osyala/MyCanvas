Yeezy Over Jumpman

Yeezy Over Jumpman, is a website that searches all of instagram for anything "Yeezy" that is for sale. We do this by using instagram public API as well as instafeed.js, which is also very simple to use. Clicking on an image will link you to the certain post, allowing you to like/comment and even Direct Message the user. Good Luck on finding a great deal on a pair of Yeezys!
